About Me

My name is Nicholas MacDonald, and I live in Gainesville, Florida! I have a background in game design, and I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Digital Arts and Sciences. I'm excited to learn new coding languages and all the things that go into web design. That is literally why I am pursuing this Master's degree. I enjoy watching television, drawing, writing, taking walks, and eating. I also greatly enjoy making and playing video and tabletop games. My most recently finished tabletop game includes a version of Mafia that can be played with up to around 200 players. As far as video games go, I am looking into modding Inscryption, and I am also trying to learn the use of websockets, so I can make party games similar to those made by Jackbox. I feel like getting a degree in Web Design will serve to hit two birds with one stone: It will give me a more reliable, primary source of income as well as the skills I need to make a site that will put my foot in the game industry's door.
